What does this mean? Here is information to put these scores into context.

EPIC-26 is a survey that captures different aspects of urinary, sexual, and bowel function in patients undergoing treatment for prostate cancer. This app predicts your long-term sexual function based on the information you provided.

A score of 0-40 means poor sexual function.

A score of 41-59 means intermediate sexual function.

A score of 60-72 means good sexual function.

A score of 73 or greater means very good sexual function.

Where do these numbers come from? The first three (0-40, 41-59, and 60+) come from Table 3 of this paper by Vertosick and colleagues. The 73+ comes from this paper by Singh and colleagues.

In addition to predicting your overall sexual function (the EPIC-26 sexual domain score), this app also predicts your long-term chance of having erections firm enough for intercourse.
